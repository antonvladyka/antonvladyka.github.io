Anton Vladyka

Anton Vladyka received his B.Sc. degree in Applied Physics from Taras Shevchenko National University of Kyiv, Ukraine, in 2010 and M.Sc. degree in 2012 (thesis work “Transport and noise characteristics of individual biomolecules in mechanically controllable break-junctions” performed in the Forschungszentrum Julich, Germany).

Joined the group in Basel in November 2012 under the project “SyMoNe”.

In March 2017 defended  his doctoral thesis entitled “Detailed analysis of single molecular junctions for novel computing architectures” and obtained PhD degree in Nanosciences.
