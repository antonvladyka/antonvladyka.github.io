Anton Vladyka, Dr.

I am an experimental physicist with Ph.D. in NanoSciences and a strong interest in doing scientific computing & scientific data analysis. Currently located on Finland.

I am fluent in Matlab and R, and I have an advanced experience in Python for scientific data analysis (numpy/scipy/pandas/matplotlib etc.), for Machine&Deep learning applications (scikit&pytorch), for GUI development (PyQt). I also like to prepare documents in LaTeX and 3D images in POV-Ray. I extensively worked with OriginLab, LabView etc.

My experimental expertise includes condensed matter phycics & molecular electronics, various microscopy techniques (SEM, TEM/AFM/STM and Raman), cleanroom experience, nanofabrication.

Apart from my work, I do like skiing & hiking, swing dancing and populating Google Maps with fancy panoramic pictures.
